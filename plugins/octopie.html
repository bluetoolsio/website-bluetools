Activate

Set a shortcut to open OctoPie from the N-panel and get instant access to eight slots you can customize with your own tools, actions, and workflows.

Overview

OctoPie lets you build your own Blender pie menus around the way you work. Create editor- and mode-aware profiles, fill slots with operators, shortcuts, scripts, assets, macros, lists, or nested pies, and keep your most-used actions within one fast gesture.

Key Features

Context Aware

Menus can change by editor and mode, including Object, Edit, Sculpt, Geometry Nodes, UV, Sequencer, Graph Editor, and more.

Slot-Based Workflows

Each pie has eight directional slots that can run operators, shortcuts, scripts, assets, macros, lists, or nested pies.

Portable Setup

Export, import, back up, and restore your OctoPie settings, scripts, icons, profiles, and keymaps.
