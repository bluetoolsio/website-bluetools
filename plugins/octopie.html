OctoPie

Context-aware pie menus, shortcuts, assets, and scripts for Blender

v1.2.0Updated April 2026
SuperhiveGumroad
Trailer Video Placeholder

Overview

A custom command surface for Blender.

OctoPie lets you build your own Blender pie menus around the way you work. Create editor- and mode-aware profiles, fill slots with operators, shortcuts, scripts, assets, macros, lists, or nested pies, and keep your most-used actions within one fast gesture.

Key Features

Build From Scratch

Create your own Blender pie menus from the ground up, then fill each eight-slot layout with the operators, shortcuts, scripts, assets, macros, lists, or nested pies you actually use.

Context-Aware Profiles

Build different profiles for Blender editors and modes, so OctoPie can surface the right commands whether you are modeling, editing UVs, working in Geometry Nodes, or managing assets.

Workflow showcase

Build the pie menu around the way you actually work.

OctoPie slots can launch operators, shortcuts, scripts, assets, macros, lists, and nested pies, so each profile becomes a fast command surface for the current Blender context.

Activate

Set a shortcut to open OctoPie from the N-panel and get instant access to eight slots you can customize with your own tools, actions, and workflows.
