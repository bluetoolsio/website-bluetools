Overview

Smart Pie radically improves your workflow in Blender by introducing context-aware pie menus that adapt to your current mode, selection, and tools. Work faster, smarter, and with less friction.

Key Features

Context Aware

Menus change automatically depending on whether you are in Object, Edit, or Sculpt mode.

Customizable

Easily add your own favorite tools, macros, and scripts directly into any pie slot.

Fast Execution

Built for speed with minimal overhead, ensuring menus pop up instantly.
